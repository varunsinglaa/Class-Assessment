GROUP
TRAINING

A personal trainer motivate clients by setting goals and providing feedback and accountability to clients. It is very important that a trainer takes their time to evaluate a clients personal life outside of the gym.

Many types of group exercise classes exist, but generally these include classes based on aerobics, cycling (spinning), boxing or martial arts.


AEROBICS

Aerobics is a form of physical exercise that combines rhythmic aerobic exercise with stretching and strength training routines with the goal of improving all elements of fitness. . Aerobics classes may allow participants to select their level of participation according to their fitness level.

H.I.I.T.

It is High Intensity Interval Training. HIIT is a cardio session arranged as short bursts of very hard work. The whole point
of high-intensity training is to kick up the intensity of your cardio. That’s why they’re short—anywhere from 20 to 90 seconds, typically.
